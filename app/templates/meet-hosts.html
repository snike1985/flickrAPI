Meet the Hosts

Lior Krengel

Head of Startup for Startup at monday.com

Lior joined monday.com to launch a giving back initiative for the startup community. Lior was formerly a Principal at Mangrove Capital Partners, a European VC, where she focused on investing in early stage startups. Her engagement and contribution to the startup ecosystem began as she served as the General Manager of the co-working space for entrepreneurship and innovation, “The Library Tel Aviv”
